Cajun Tartar Sauce

This Cajun tartar sauce, made with Old Bay and dill pickle relish, takes tartar sauce in a new direciton. It's great on fried fish, and also as a dipping sauce for French fries.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup mayonnaise
  • 2 teaspoon seafood seasoning, such as Old Bay
  • 1/4 cup dill pickle relish
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • 2 teaspoons lemon juice
  • 1/4 teaspoon freshlhy ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Place mayonnaise, Old Bay, relish, onion powder, lemon juice, and pepper in a bowl.
  2. Stir until combined.
  3. Cover and refrigerate for at least 1 hour before serving.
